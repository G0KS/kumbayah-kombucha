What is Kombucha?

Kombucha is a fermented, lightly effervescent, sweetened black tea drink commonly consumed for its purported health benefits. Sometimes the beverage is called kombucha tea to distinguish it from the culture of bacteria and yeast. Juice, spices, fruit or other flavorings are often added.

About Us

Kumbayah Kombucha is a probiotic tea that has been fermented with SCOBY (A colony of good bacteria & yeast). It is then flavoured with fresh fruits & herbs resulting in healthy & delicious sparkling beverage. Kumbayah Kombucha stands as a Healthy Soda alternative due to low sugar & Natural carbonation. It is full of Probiotics & Antioxidants which Helps digestion, Improves mood,skin & detoxifies the liver.

When I was in Bhopal, a good friend of mine from Spain gifted me a kombucha scoby, which is a starter kit to brew your own concoction. I soon started brewing my own kombucha as it helped me with my stomach issues. The owner of Cafe Onway in Palayam, Trivandrum and I used to chill during closing hours and share a bottle or two of my kombucha with him. He liked it so much that he offered it to his customers. They gave really good reviews. That's when I decided to sell my product.

- Arvind (Founder)
